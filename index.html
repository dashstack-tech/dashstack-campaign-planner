Deduplication System

3-layer protection — prevent same lead getting multiple emails

1
Layer 1 — Apify Level (Before Scraping)
Never scrape same City × Industry combination twice. Use the city chips in the Scraping tab to track what's done. Rule: if chip is marked done, never run that city+industry again on Apify.
2
Layer 2 — Google Sheet Master List (Before Upload)
Create a Google Sheet called "DashStack Master Leads" with columns: Email | Company | Industry | City | Scraped Date | Uploaded Date | Campaign.

Every time you export CSV from Apify → paste into master sheet first → use this formula to find duplicates before uploading to Smartlead:

=COUNTIF($B$2:B2,B2)>1
Add this in column H ("IsDuplicate"). Filter H=TRUE and delete those rows before uploading.
3
Layer 3 — Smartlead Global Unsubscribe List
In Smartlead → Settings → Global Unsubscribe → upload every email you've ever contacted. This prevents the same person receiving emails from 2 different mailboxes/campaigns. Update this list every Friday with that week's sends.
4
Layer 4 — Follow-up Dedup (Smartlead Automatic)
Smartlead automatically skips follow-ups for anyone who replied or unsubscribed. Your sequence is: Day 0 (initial) → Day 7 (follow-up 1) → Day 12 (final follow-up). Anyone who replied gets removed from sequence automatically. You don't need to do anything extra.
⚠️ Weekly Dedup Checklist (Every Friday):
□ Export this week's sent list from Smartlead
□ Paste emails into Master Sheet
□ Upload to Smartlead Global Unsubscribe list
□ Mark scraped city chips as done in Scraping tab
